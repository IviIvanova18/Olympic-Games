Exploring the Olympic Games

Delve into the rich history of the Olympic Games. Discover patterns, trends, and stories through interactive data visualizations.

Visualization 1: Medal Summary by Country

Explore the total number of medals won by each country in the Olympic Games, spanning from 1896 to 2022. Gain insights into the dominant sporting nations and their historic achievements. By clicking on the rings, you can zoom in on the respective continent. You can also select a specific edition of the games using the timeline below or a certain medal type (gold, silver, bronze).

Using the interactive drop-down menu you can chose the game season and the desired sport event within. The medal count for the selected sports will be represented on the map.
